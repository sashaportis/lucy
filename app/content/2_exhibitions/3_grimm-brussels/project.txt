Text: 

GRIMM is pleased to announce an exhibition in Brussels. In a gallery space across the street from BOZAR we will present a group exhibition with selected highlights from our gallery program. 

The opening will take place during the official Art Brussels Gallery Night on April 18th and the exhibition will be on view until May 5th.
